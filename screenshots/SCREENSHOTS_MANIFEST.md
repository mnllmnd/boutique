## Screenshot Specifications for App Stores

### Google Play Store Requirements
- **Minimum:** 2 screenshots, **Maximum:** 8 screenshots
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Minimum dimension:** 320px
- **Maximum dimension:** 3840px
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16, 16:9, or 1:1
- **File size:** Max 8MB per image

**Recommended Setup for Boutique:**
- Use all 5 screenshots (1-5 above)
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- Order: 1 (Dashboard) → 2 (Create) → 3 (History) → 4 (Detail) → 5 (Settings)

### Apple App Store Requirements
- **Count:** 2-5 screenshots per language
- **Format:** PNG only
- **Dimensions:** 1242x2688 (iPhone 14 Pro Max) or 1170x2532 (iPhone 13)
- **File size:** Max 5MB per image

**For Boutique (iPhone):**
- Current 1080x1920 screenshots can be resized to 1170x2532
- Use same 5 screenshots in same order
